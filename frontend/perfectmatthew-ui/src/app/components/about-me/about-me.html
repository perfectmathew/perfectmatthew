About Me

Hello it's me Matthew I come from a charming town Głuchołazy in Poland. My adventure with programming began in my youth, driven by curiosity and a desire to create. From my fascination with writing my own plugins for the Minecraft server to configuring and developing the Metin2 server, this early stage shaped my passion for code.

I've always had a head full of ideas, and now, thanks to creating this space, I share them and my work with the world. Besides programming, my favorite form of relaxation is a good read, both in traditional form and as an audiobook.
